Research

Pain Point: Most of Thrift stores have terrible quality and have too much stuff to dig through.
Pleasure: Giving a new life to old items and helping out the environment are key components of thrifting

View details »

Ideation

We believe creating an online thrift ecommerce platform for everyday shoppers looking for an alternative way to buy and sell used goods, and/or shoppers looking for great products at discounted prices, will bring a much desired substitue to the current thrift shopping experience.

View details »

Design

Our design focus started from our competitors analysis. We realized that not many application allowed users to find a specific materials that they want while giving them an "element of suprise" when discovering a new item.
